Detection of aftershocks using AI

Jun 20, 2022
1 min read
Python

Advisor Teacher:Zefeng Li .
June 2021 - Present

Aim

Combining artificial intelligence and new methods of signal processing to improve the aftershock detection capability within 1 hour after an earthquake.

Method

Training a net to separate superimposed seismic waveforms from the same area one year after the Ridgecrest earthquake, based on PhaseNet. Useing this net to recognize small aftershock waveforms after earthquakes
